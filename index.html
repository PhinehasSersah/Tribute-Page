A brief overview of the life of Jerry John Rawlings


  • 1947 - Jerry Rawlings John was born on in Accra

  • 1967 - Finished his secondary education at Achimota College

  • 1968 - He joined the Ghana Air Force and was posted to Takoradi

  • 1969 - Graduated and was commissioned as a pilot officer, winning the coveted "Speed Bird Trophy" as the best cadet in flying the Su-7 ground attack supersonic jet aircraft

  • 1978 - He involved himself with the student community of the University of Ghana, where he developed a more leftist ideology through reading and discussion of social and political ideas
